Title: Bread pakoda in air fryer
Description: Bread Pakoda or bread fritters is a famous Indian Street food made with stuffed bread coated with spiced gram flour batter and then deep fried.
Source: blessmyfoodbypayal
Original URL: https://blessmyfoodbypayal.com/bread-pakoda-in-air-fryer/
Yield: 10,10 Pakodas
Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 30 minutes
Cookbook: First Cookbook
Section: indian
Image: http://blessmyfoodbypayal.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/IMG_0378.png
Ingredients: 
	3 medium boiled potatoes
	1 tsp cumin seeds
	2 green chilies ((finely chopped))
	1 tsp ginger ((crushed))
	1/2 tsp garam masala
	1/2 tsp coriander powder
	Red chilli powder to taste
	Salt to taste
	1 tbsp coriander leaves ((roughly chopped))
	240 ml besan/gram flour (roasted, if making pakoda in air fryer)
	1 tsp ajwain/carom seeds
	1/8 tsp turmeric powder
	1 tbsp coriander leaves (roughly chopped)
	Water as required
	Bread slices as required
	Oil for greasing
Instructions: 
	MAKING OF STUFFING:
	Take potatoes in a bowl and mash them.
	Add rest of the ingredients i.e. cumin seeds, green chilies, ginger, garam masala, coriander powder, red chilli powder, salt and coriander leaves. Mix well.
	MAKING OF BATTER:
	Take gram flour in separate bowl and add carom seeds, turmeric and coriander leaves. Mix.
	Add water little by little to make a batter. Batter needs to be slightly thin if cooking in air fryer otherwise keep it thick if deep frying.
	MAKING OF PAKODA:
	Cut bread slices into halves giving them a diagonal shape.
	Place potato stuffing on a bread and place another slice of bread making it a sandwich.
	Dip the sandwich in batter from both the sides.
	Arrange aluminium foil (with holes) in an air fryer basket and grease it with oil.
	Place bread slices in the basket.
	Air fry @180°C for 15-20 minutes. Do turn the sides during the half time.

Title: Seeded bread and roast tomato soup
Description: 
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 
Cookbook: First Cookbook
Section: soup
Image: 
Ingredients: 
	500g vine tomatoes
	2 cloves of garlic
	1 bunch fresh basil
	olive oil
	2 400g tins tomatoes
	4 slices of seeded bread
Instructions: 
	preheat oven to 220C
	put vine tomatoes in a dish with garlic, half the basil, salt and pepper and drizzle with olive oil. Roast for 20 mins.
	Take out of the oven, put on the hob, add tinned tomatoes and a tin of water, break up with a spoon then cook for 20 minutes.
	tear bread and basil over the top, leave to sit for a few minutes, drizzle with olive oil.
